Community Guidelines

Last Updated: July 22, 2026

IconBuilds reviews, reports, usernames, support requests, and other community content should stay honest, helpful, safe, and respectful.

Respectful Conduct

Harassment, threats, hate speech, slurs, impersonation, excessive profanity, scams, spam, malicious links, and personal information are not allowed.

Reviews and Reports

Reviews should be relevant to the resource and based on real experience. False reports, fake reviews, rating manipulation, and abusive reporting may lead to restrictions.

Marketplace Integrity

Users may not redistribute protected resources, abuse downloads or refunds, manipulate marketplace statistics, or attempt unauthorized access to IconBuilds systems.
